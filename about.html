Me (On the left)

I was born in South Carolina (I have no memories of the state) and raised in Tucson, Arizona. In 2012, I graduated from Catalina Foothills High School. Afterwards, I began pursuing my B.S. in electrical engineering and computer science at the University of California, Berkeley. I plan to graduate with my B.S. in May, 2016.

Outside of courses and research, I invest most of my time into food. I am an avid home cook and baker who enjoys exploring the well established world of food. Though I am still working on most of the fundamentals that any competent chef should master, I hope to one day have the intuition and skill to write and execute my own recipes.

Overall, I enjoy playing tennis, drinking coffee and eating at new places.

Once upon a time, I also learned to blacksmith at The Crucible and I would love to get back into it to work on a collaborative project.

So if you share any of these interests, do not hesitate to contact me so we can arrange to meet up!
