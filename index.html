Introduction

Currently, I am pursuing my graduate studies under the supervision of Professor Karl Henrik Johansson at the Division of Decision and Control Systems of the school of EECS at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden.

Recently, I worked as a Machine Learning and Robotics Engineer at OffWorld, where I now act as a Machine Learning and Controls Research Consultant.

This website primarily serves as a brief overview of my work. However, I also sometimes use this website to post or link content I find interesting or useful. The topics I am currently most interested in are system and control theory for large-scale cyber-physical systems, intelligent transportation, and the design of safety-critical robotic systems.
